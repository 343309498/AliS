Who Is Scottie Pippen?

Scotty Maurice Pippen Sr., usually spelt Scottie Pippen who used to play in the NBA. He played 17 seasons while playing for 2 teams his entire career and those 2 teams were The Portland Trail Blazers and The Chicago Bulls.

Total Games Played

Scottie Pippen played a total of 1178 games during his entire NBA Career.

Scottie Pippens Career Stats

Scottie Pippen had a total of 18940 total points with 6135 assists and 7494 rebounds.

Career Awards Won

Here are some awards that Scottie Pippen won during his career.

  • 2 Time Gold Medal winner with the Olympic Dream team in 1992 and 1996
  • 8 Time All Defensive First Team
  • 1 Time All Star Game MVP
  • 6 Time NBA Champion
